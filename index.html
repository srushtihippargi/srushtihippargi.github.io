About

I'm a Master's student in Automotive Engineering at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, focusing on autonomous vehicle perception, control, and 3D scene understanding. My research revolves around probabilistic representations for semantic scene completion, Gaussian Splatting, and SLAM‑aware learning methods.

Previously, I engineered multi‑sensor pipelines that fuse LiDAR, camera, and radar data for robust 3D object tracking. I am passionate about building real‑time robotics systems that close the loop between perception and planning.
